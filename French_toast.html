Recipe instructions
  1. Beat the eggs first, add milk and sugar, and stir until the sugar melts.
  2. Soak the thick slices of toast on both sides for about 3-5 minutes to allow the toast to soak up.
  3. Put the cream in the pot, add the soaked toast, and fry slowly over "medium-low heat" until golden brown on both sides.
  4. Place the fruit and honey on the plate, and finally sprinkle with powdered sugar to complete the "French toast".
  5. Finish!
