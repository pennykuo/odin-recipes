Panna cotta

Ingredientsï¼š

whole milk 500cc, corn flour 60g, little condensed milk.

Recipe instructions
  1. Pour the fresh milk into the pan over medium-low heat, stirring and pouring in the cornmeal.
  2. Add condensed milk (or other types of sugar to your liking) and stir constantly.
  3. When the liquid thickens, turn to low heat and continue stirring until smooth, then turn off the heat.
  4. Pour it directly into the container without cooling, and spread baking paper (plastic wrap and lid are also acceptable) on top and refrigerate for 3 hours.
  5. Finish!
