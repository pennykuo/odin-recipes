Penny's kitchen

Ingredients：

Salt, baking powder, milk, flour, regular sugar, one egg, butter, chocolate chips, a banana.

Recipe instructions
  1. Sift flour, baking powder, regular sugar, and salt.
  2. Add in egg, milk, and melted butter, then mix.
  3. Set the stove heat to a low temperature. And pour in the batter.
  4. When you see a lot of little air bubbles coming to the top, it’s time to flip.
  5. Decorate their cute little faces!
  6. Finish!
